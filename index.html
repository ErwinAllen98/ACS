History & Risks (Adjusted)

The risk factors in the "History & Risks" tab are derived from the landmark INTERHEART study. This global case-control study used multivariate logistic regression to isolate the independent impact of each risk factor. The published Adjusted Odds Ratios (AORs) were converted to Adjusted Likelihood Ratios (LRs).

Key Principle: Because these LRs are statistically independent, they can be multiplied together to calculate a cumulative risk.

Pain & Physical Findings (Adjusted)

To ensure statistical consistency, all findings in the "Pain Characteristics" and "Physical Findings" sections are sourced from a single, high-quality prospective validation study by Gencer et al. The study used multivariable logistic regression to identify independent predictors of ACS. This guarantees that all clinical findings are mutually adjusted and can be multiplied together without double-counting risk.
