Clinical Scope & Intended Use

This tool is a clinical decision support system, not a substitute for clinical judgment. Please confirm you understand its intended use.

Inclusion Criteria (Use For)

  • Adult patients in an acute care setting (e.g., Emergency Department).
  • Presenting with symptoms suggestive of ACS (e.g., chest pain, dyspnea).
  • Diagnostic uncertainty exists, and ACS is on the differential.

Exclusion Criteria (Do NOT Use For)

  • Obvious STEMI on ECG: Patient needs immediate reperfusion, not risk scoring.
  • Hemodynamically Unstable Patients: Prioritize resuscitation.
  • Clear, High-Risk Alternative Diagnoses: (e.g., Aortic Dissection, PE).
  • Obvious Non-Cardiac Cause: (e.g., Shingles, Rib Fracture).
